Click on the map button and the map will load. It may take a little while, but it will fully appear. You can click on any of the buttons ( ex. Bakeries, Libraries, etc.) and the map will automaticallly show locations of the category selected. To move the map around, you'll need to use the mouse/touchpad to find locations for other places.

Click on a marker in order to make its description appear. You can also click on any of the names on the sidebar and you'll be taken its location.
To zoom into the map drag two fingers down on the touchpad, to zoom out you do the opposite. If you're using a mouse, use the scroller to zoom in or out.
