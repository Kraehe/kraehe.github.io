About Exploradora

Exploradora is a tool that can help both tourists and locals alike find niche spots in their area, like cute bakeries or libraries. It's especially important for people in large cities to see green spaces within the concrete jungles they're in. Seeing nature is extremely beneficial to our health, and we want people to go out and explore. We hope our website fulfills this purpose and encourages people to go out and explore their surroundings.
